    Rate this App

    Worms became a trendsetter due to its originality and addictive gameplay.

    Wormux is an adaptation of that game for the Linux platform, which is also free and open source.

    The protagonists are the legendary Linux mascots like Tux and Konqui, alongside others related to the world of Unix and free software.

    In addition, you can use a wide-ranging arsenal of all types of crazy weapons.

    Wormux also includes an online game mode where you can battle other users online or through a local network.
